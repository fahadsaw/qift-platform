# Merchant Requirements (Bulk Fulfillment Addendum checklist)

Per pilot merchant, before any campaign references them:

## Commercial
- [ ] Volume unit pricing locked pre-campaign (no mid-window changes)
- [ ] Capacity commitment in writing: N units, delivery window, cities
- [ ] Shortfall clause: backup-merchant trigger + per-unit penalty
- [ ] Substitution ladder pre-approved by the company (equal-or-better only)
- [ ] Invoice Qift per fulfilled batch; paid NET-7 after delivery confirmation

## Operational
- [ ] Daily stock attestation during claim windows
- [ ] Delivery SLA: within the agreed window of receiving the fulfillment list
- [ ] Proof of delivery REQUIRED per order (photo or OTP-at-door)
- [ ] Daily status updates (delivered/failed per recipient)
- [ ] Failed-address flow: report to Qift ops only — NEVER contact the company
- [ ] Replace-first policy on damage (reconcile money after)
- [ ] Packaging standard agreed; branded insert handling if elected (Pro+)

## Privacy (contractual)
- [ ] Addresses used solely for this delivery
- [ ] Deletion after delivery confirmation (confirmed in writing at D+30)
- [ ] No marketing to recipients, ever
- [ ] No disclosure of the client company unless branded packaging was elected
