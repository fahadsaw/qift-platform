# Company Requirements (what we need / what we promise)

## We need from the company
1. **Signatures**: Pilot Services Agreement + DPA before any data moves.
2. **Prepayment** before dispatch (bank transfer against our invoice).
3. **Roster**: name, mobile (E.164 or local), optional email, optional
   group/department — via the secured channel only. ≤500 rows.
   NO addresses (we will drop them), NO IDs, NO extra columns.
4. **Roster accuracy ownership**: the company signs off the validated
   list; wrong numbers are flagged back but remain their data to fix.
5. **A named campaign owner** reachable during the claim window.
6. **Pre-announcement** to staff before D0 (template provided) — this
   single step roughly doubles SMS trust.
7. **Gift + copy approval** on the locked spec (no edits post-approval).
8. **Acceptance of the leaver rule**: claimed gifts are the
   recipient's, irrevocably; unclaimed rows are voidable on request.
9. **A covenant not to request addresses** — contractual mirror of the
   structural rule.

## We promise the company
- Live funnel (names + statuses ONLY; non-participation shown as one
  undifferentiated bucket — no decline/opt-out distinction, ever).
- Re-send + long-tail calls; unreached names returned for internal nudge.
- ZATCA-compliant invoice; gift cost charged on CLAIMED, platform fee
  per contract; final reconciliation at close.
- Support line for their employees during the window (Arabic-first).
- D+30 purge with certificate; processor posture under the DPA.
- Completion report within 3 working days of final delivery.
