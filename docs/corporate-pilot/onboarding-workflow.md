# Company Onboarding Workflow

| # | Step | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Qualification call (30 min) — size, occasion, cities, budget/head, decision map | Founder | Go / no-go note |
| 2 | Privacy walkthrough — the one-way-glass page | Founder | Their HR + legal nod |
| 3 | Send Pilot Services Agreement + DPA | Founder | Signed pack |
| 4 | Commercials — quote (gift × N + fee × N + VAT), pilot discount w/ case-study clause | Founder | PO / prepayment received |
| 5 | Named contacts exchanged (their campaign owner ↔ our ops runner) | Ops | Contact sheet |
| 6 | Roster handover via the secured channel (NEVER email body / chat) | Ops | Roster in the vault |
| 7 | Roster validation report (dedupe, E.164, liveness flags, name-similarity warnings) | Ops | Company sign-off on final list |
| 8 | Gift selection from bulk-ready catalog + message template approval (AR-first, preview verbatim) | Founder + Ops | Locked campaign spec |
| 9 | Merchant capacity locked in writing | Merchant coord. | Capacity commitment |
| 10 | Employer pre-announcement sent internally ("a gift is coming via Qift") | Company | Trust primed |
| 11 | → hand off to `pilot-checklist.md` D-3 rehearsal | Ops | |

Rules: no dispatch before prepayment clears (step 4) and sign-off
(step 7). Any address column in the roster is DROPPED at step 7 and
the company is told why.
