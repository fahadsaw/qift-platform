# Sales Deck — Qift Business Pilot (founder-delivered)

## Narrative arc (10 slides, ≤15 minutes)

1. **The pain** — "How did your last Eid gifting actually go?"
   Spreadsheets, address collection over WhatsApp, one vendor, no
   tracking, HR chased people for weeks. (Let them tell it; they will.)
2. **The promise** — Your team's gifts, delivered to their doors,
   without you ever collecting a single address.
3. **How it works** (one diagram): you upload names + numbers → we
   send "وصلتك هدية من {شركتك} عبر قِفت" → each person confirms
   their own address privately → gifts arrive → you watch the live
   funnel. You never see addresses. They never download anything.
4. **The privacy slide** (the one that closes deals): one-way glass
   diagram — company sees names + statuses only; merchant sees one
   delivery at a time; everything purges 30 days after the campaign
   with a certificate. We act as your data processor under a DPA.
5. **The recipient experience** — phone screenshots of the real SMS
   + claim flow. Under 60 seconds, Arabic-first, no app.
6. **What's in the catalog** — curated GCC corporate staples (dates,
   chocolate, perfume) from reviewed merchants with committed
   capacity. Company approves the exact gift.
7. **The dashboard** — dispatched → claimed → delivered funnel,
   re-send to unreached, names-and-status only.
8. **Pricing** — gift cost (transparent, no markup) + per-recipient
   platform fee (SAR 15–25 list, pilot discount 40–50% in exchange
   for a case-study clause) + VAT. ZATCA-compliant invoice. Prepaid.
9. **Pilot terms** — ≤500 recipients, ≥10 working days runway, one
   campaign, we staff a support line for your people, you get a
   completion report + purge certificate.
10. **Ask** — pick the occasion, pick the gift, send us the roster.

## Objection handling
- "Why not our usual vendor?" → They deliver boxes to your office;
  we deliver moments to homes — and your HR does nothing.
- "Data safety?" → Slide 4; offer the DPA before they ask.
- "Employees will think it's phishing" → Employer pre-announcement
  template included; registered sender; support line in the SMS.
- "What if people don't respond?" → Re-send + call the long tail;
  unreached names returned to you; you only pay gift cost on claimed.
- "Can we see where they live?" → No — structurally. That's the
  feature. (Say it exactly that confidently.)

## Qualification (disqualify politely)
50–300 employees · KSA cities with merchant coverage · decision-maker
reachable (CHRO/founder/admin-affairs) · occasion within 6 weeks ·
NOT a bank/government entity (those wait for round 3 with case studies).
